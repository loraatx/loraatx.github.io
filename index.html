About LoRaATX

LoRaATX.City is the website for the Kickstarter campaign to develop three case study demonstrations of possible uses for a long-range IoT network in Austin.

The campaign seeks 12k to develop: 1) a pet tracker, 2) an inline scooter, and 3) a portable flood warning system, all activated by LoRaWAN gateways.

This project is a proof-of-concept for a city-owned LoRaWAN network in Austin—a long-range, low-power wireless system that lets smart devices like scooters, pet trackers, leak sensors, and air monitors connect without using cellular data. Instead of relying on expensive telecoms, this Kickstarter funds a demo showing how Austin could operate its own secure, low-frequency IoT network as a public utility. We’ll equip a scooter with LoRaWAN tech, turn an everyday pet collar into a pet tracker with a LoRaWAN transmitter, and use a terrarium to demonstrate a LoRaWAN flood alert system. The Kickstarter also funds a time-limited small nonprofit for possible federal grants and to organize stakeholders of the idea. The campaign will be conducted in August, 2025.
