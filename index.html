About LoRaATX

LoRaATX.City is the website for the kickstarter campaign to develop 3 case study demonstrations of possible uses for a long range IoT network in Austin

The campaign seeks 12k to develop 1. A pet tracker, 2. An Inline Scooter and 3. A portable flood warning system all activated by LoRaWAN gateways

his project is a proof-of-concept for a city-owned LoRaWAN network in Austin—a long-range, low-power wireless system that lets smart devices like scooters, pet trackers, leak sensors, and air monitors connect without using cellular data. Instead of relying on expensive telecoms, this Kickstarter funds a demo showing how Austin could operate its own secure, low-frequency IoT network as a public utility. We’ll equip a scooter with LoRaWAN tech, turn a everyday pet collar into a pet tracker with a LoRaWAN transmitter, and use a terrarium to demonstrate a LoRaWAN Flood Alert system. The kickstarter also funds a time limited small nonprofit for possible federal grants and to organize stakeholders of the idea. The campaign will be conducted in August, 2025
