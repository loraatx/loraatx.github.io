Parcelizer

Explore Austin parcel boundaries. Click any parcel to look up its zoning and metadata from Supabase. Tiles are streamed from a PMTiles archive; attributes come from the parcels table on click.

Zoom in to view parcels

Visualization layer: PMTiles served from Supabase Storage. System of record: public.parcels in Supabase Postgres + PostGIS. Schema includes RAG-ready stubs (parcel_documents, parcel_embeddings) for later semantic enrichment.
