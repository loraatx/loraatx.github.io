Overview

This report compiles verified information on bicycle and e-bike shops in the Greater Austin, Texas area, focusing on central Austin through Round Rock, Cedar Park, and Pflugerville.12 It emphasizes shops that sell e-bikes, notes which are independent versus chain/corporate, and highlights those with professional bike fitting and connections to the Austin Energy E-Ride rebate program.34

The report is structured around two key planning artifacts: a shop-level table with attributes relevant to purchasing and fitting, and a sample of current e-bike prices by brand and model from shops that publish inventory online.56

Austin Energy E-Ride Rebate Context

Austin Energy’s Electric Ride (E-Ride) Rebate program offers incentives for eligible e-bikes and other small electric vehicles purchased from participating local dealerships.37 The utility specifies that qualifying purchases must come from Austin Energy–approved participating dealerships with a local storefront.

Public-facing documentation for the E-Ride program describes rebate tiers by vehicle type and cost but does not always provide a straightforward, up-to-date HTML list of every participating bike dealer.34 Third-party coverage and community reports fill some gaps but should be cross-checked directly with Austin Energy for purchases.89

Shops Without Full Online E-Bike Price Grids

Several Austin-area shops known for road, triathlon, or high-end builds do not maintain a comprehensive, public-facing e-bike price list.28121418 For planning, they are still important, especially given their fitting expertise and potential access to select e-bike models via brand partnerships.

- Mellow Johnny's Bike Shop: Strong reputation for high-end road and MTB sales and professional fitting; e-bikes are present but not broken out in a dedicated online catalog.1112 - Austin Tri Cyclist: Focus on triathlon and performance road bikes; site content centers on non-motorized bikes.1415 - Velorangutan: Custom builds and boutique brands with an emphasis on analog/performance bikes; e-bike offerings, if any, are not clearly listed.2829 - Blur Cycleworks: General shop in Round Rock with limited online SKU-level detail; phone or in-person visits are required to assess e-bike stock.3233

Planning Considerations for E-Bike Purchases

When using this report for planning e-bike purchases or rebate-eligible acquisitions in Greater Austin, a few practical considerations apply:

- Rebate eligibility must be confirmed at purchase time: Austin Energy’s E-Ride program requires purchases from participating dealers, and these lists can change; buyers should verify the current dealer list or call Austin Energy directly.34 - Inventory and pricing are fluid: E-bike inventories, especially at premium shops such as Electric Avenue and Trek Parmer, change frequently, and sale prices may be time-limited.56 - Fit and use case matter as much as price: Shops like Mellow Johnny’s, Austin Tri Cyclist, and REI Gateway offer professional or semi-professional bike fitting, which can be critical for comfort and performance, especially on higher-end e-bikes.241214 - Geographic distribution across the metro: Central Austin hosts the densest cluster of high-end and specialty e-bike retailers (Electric Avenue, MOD BIKES, Mellow Johnny’s, Austin Tri Cyclist), while North Austin and suburbs (Trek Parmer, Specialized Domain, Blur Cycleworks) provide options closer to Cedar Park, Round Rock, and Pflugerville.19322151

Taken together, the shop list, coordinates CSV, and sample pricing tables provide a planning-ready foundation for mapping, price benchmarking, and rebate-eligible e-bike purchasing strategies in Greater Austin.
